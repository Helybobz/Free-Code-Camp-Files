Delicious Nigerian Puff Puff

This recipe will introduce you to the enticing world of nigerian pastries. Puff puff is a staple easy-to-make delicacy straight from the continent. It is soft and comforting, ideal to use a snack at your house parties or weekend chill

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) of Plain Flour
  • 2 teaspoons of yeast (the one used in baking bread)
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ cup granulated sugar (or to your taste)
  • 3 pinches of salt
  • Lukewarm water (for mixing)
  • Vegetable Oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Activate the yeast (if not instant)
  2. Mix the dry ingredients
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
  4. Allow the batter to rise
  5. Heat the oil
  6. Shape and fry the puff-puff
  7. Cook until golden brown
  8. Drain and serve
